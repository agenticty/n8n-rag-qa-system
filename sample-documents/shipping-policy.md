# Shipping and Returns Policy

## Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping
- **Cost:** Free on orders over $50, otherwise $7.99
- **Delivery Time:** 5-7 business days
- **Tracking:** Included
- **Carrier:** USPS or UPS (our choice)

### Express Shipping
- **Cost:** $19.99
- **Delivery Time:** 2-3 business days
- **Tracking:** Real-time tracking included
- **Carrier:** UPS or FedEx

### Overnight Shipping
- **Cost:** $39.99
- **Delivery Time:** Next business day (orders placed before 2 PM EST)
- **Tracking:** Real-time tracking included
- **Carrier:** FedEx or UPS

- **Canada:** $14.99, 7-10 business days

**Note:** International customers are responsible for customs duties and import taxes.

## Returns Policy

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund on all products within 30 days of delivery if:
- Product is in original condition
- All accessories and packaging are included
- Product is not damaged or modified

### Return Process
1. Contact customer support at returns@smarthomehub.com
2. Receive a Return Authorization (RA) number
3. Pack the item securely with all accessories
4. Ship to our returns center (address provided with RA number)
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days of receipt

### Return Shipping Costs
- **Defective products:** We cover return shipping
- **Change of mind:** Customer pays return shipping
- **Wrong item sent:** We cover return shipping and send correct item at no charge

## Exchanges

We gladly exchange products for a different model or color within 30 days. Exchange shipping is free if:
- The original product was defective
- We sent the wrong item
- You're upgrading to a more expensive model (pay the difference)

We cannot ship to:
- PO Boxes (for SmartHome Hub hardware)

- Freight forwarders
