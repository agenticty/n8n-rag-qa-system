# Product Information

## Overview
Our flagship product is the SmartHome Hub 3000, an AI-powered home automation controller that integrates with over 500 smart home devices.

## Features
- Voice control with natural language processing
- Machine learning-based automation rules
- Energy usage monitoring and optimization
- 24/7 security monitoring
- Mobile app for iOS and Android

## Technical Specifications
- Processor: Quad-core ARM Cortex-A72
- Memory: 4GB RAM
- Storage: 32GB eMMC
- Connectivity: WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, Zigbee, Z-Wave
- Power: 5V/3A USB-C
- Dimensions: 120mm × 120mm × 25mm
- Weight: 185g

## Compatibility
Works with:
- Amazon Alexa devices
- Google Home ecosystem
- Apple HomeKit
- Samsung SmartThings
- Philips Hue
- Nest thermostats
- Ring doorbells
- And 500+ other devices

## Setup Instructions
1. Plug in the SmartHome Hub using the included USB-C cable
2. Download the SmartHome app from App Store or Google Play
3. Create an account or sign in
4. Follow the in-app pairing instructions
5. Begin adding your smart devices
